My top 3 Movies of all-time

Interstellar

Interstellar is a science-fiction movie directed by Christopher Nolan that explores space travel, time, and love. It follows a group of astronauts who journey through a wormhole to find a new home for humanity while showing how time passes differently in space. The film combines emotional storytelling with mind-blowing science concepts
